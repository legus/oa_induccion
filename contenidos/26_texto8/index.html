Y el estado, ¿Cómo ve las competencias genéricas?

El Icfes plantea el concepto de las Competencias Genéricas así:


What do we mean by “generic skills”?

Context independent

  • not tied to a particular domain of knowledge;
  • independent of particular curriculum boundaries;
  • independent of skills specific to particular occupations or industries
  • Learned and practiced within particular contexts, but transferable from one context to another
  • Complex combinations of cognitive and affective elements

e.g: “planning and organizing”, “working in a team”
